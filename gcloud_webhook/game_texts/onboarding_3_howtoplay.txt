HOW TO PLAY

💬 *Type in chat* - Talk to suspects by typing your questions

👥 *Talk to everyone* - Just type here in chat! Everyone hears you. (It also works when you are looking at case materials). 

↩️ *Reply to characters* - You can also use Telegram's reply function to answer to any character's message (like you could do in a normal group chat)

👤 *Private talk* - Use menu to choose one person, then type. 💡 People share more secrets privately.

🗂️ *Case Materials* - Check evidence and Detective Guide with investigation tips

✍️ *Learning Menu* – Check your progress and chose a text difficulty (Light / Balanced / Advanced)
